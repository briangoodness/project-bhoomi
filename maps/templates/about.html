About Project Bhoomi

test

Project Bhoomi is intended as a contribution to the world of International Development. Traditional sources of information, such as census data, are often out of date in the developing world and thus are not representative of current conditions. This paucity of infomration has afforded an opportunity for researchers to explore the possibility of supplementing traditional data sources with new forms of digital data.
This project aims to build upon work done by a team of researchers led by Neal Jean, a PhD student at Stanford. Jean has demonstrated the power of applying machine learning algorithms on satellite imagery in order to determine economic conditions in Sub Saharan Africa. We have worked to reproduce Jean's work, and to create an interactive information portal that could be used by stakeholders and decision makers in the Internation Development and Internation Aid arenas.
